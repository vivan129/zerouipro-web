Features

FAQ

Common questions.

Does it work offline? +
Yes. Voice recognition runs locally using Whisper AI. The AI intent engine requires an internet connection for the free tier, but Pro users get a cached local model option.
Which Mac models are supported? +
ZeroUI Pro works on any Mac running macOS 13 Ventura or later. It's optimized for Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, M4) but runs on Intel Macs too.
Is my voice data private? +
Completely. Voice recognition happens on your Mac using Whisper — nothing is sent to any server. Your commands stay on your machine.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, cancel anytime from your account dashboard. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
What's the refund policy? +
7-day money back guarantee, no questions asked. Just email us and we'll refund you same day.
Does it work with any app? +
Yes. ZeroUI uses macOS accessibility APIs and keyboard simulation so it works with every app — not just ones with special integrations.
